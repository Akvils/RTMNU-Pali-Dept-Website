History

  • Ph.D
  • Merit
  • H.O.D
  • Lists:

The Department of Pali-Prakrit is one of the prominent departments of the Nagpur University established in 1958. The most significant fact of this department is that, it was established after the great conversion of Dr. B. R. Ambedkar in 1956. He converted millions of people into Buddhism. For the study and teaching of these people, Nagpur University took initiative and started this department. Our department offers good research facilities to post graduation. Under this department, M.A. in Pali, M.A. in Buddhist Studies, M.Phil in Pali and Buddhism are taught. Many students have been awarded M.Phil and Ph.D degrees from this department. Many Ph.D students are taking advantage of National Fellowship like JRF, SRF, RGF, MAKAF etc. Many thinkers and scholars of National and International repute visited the department.
