About the city

Nagpur though it is known for oranges, it is the ancient city, celebrates its association with the great Naga tribe who were hard core supporter of lord Buddha. Nagpur also enjoys its historic place be being the capital of Gond dynasty and later Bhonslas ruled over it. Remains of Buddha and ancient educational centers of Buddhist era have also been found in and around Nagpur. Some of the prominent heritage sites are Pauni, Adum, Mansar, Nagarjuna Hills, Nagardhan and Bhandak. Therefore, it is one of the most popular Buddhist pilgrims and tourist places of India.

Tourist places in and around Nagpur

  • Dragon Palace Temple (Kamptee)
